About Balanced News

The ever more polarizing political climate of the United States (and by extension, the world as a whole) makes it extremely difficult for today's generation of citizens to find unbiased sources of news. Moreover, in their quest to maximize revenue, tech-based news services (including Google, Facebook, YouTube, and others) tend to personalize content recommendations for their users, causing even the most open-minded to get stuck in the trap of being exposed to only one side of a story.


Enter Balanced News™. Balanced News™ was founded in 2020 with the aim of solving the problem of echo chambers by presenting users with news from both sides of the political spectrum in a single, well-organized space. Articles drawn from multiple sources, representing both liberal and conservative opinions, and covering a variety of hot topics are presented as equals on the News page in form of clickable cards.


The News site takes about 10-15 seconds to load. Not enough time to read an article? No worries! Hovering over a card will reveal a summary of the story condensed into a few short sentences*.
